Rainwater is collected on the roof and/or in tanks and transported with gutters to a storage reservoir, where it provides water at the point of consumption or can be used for recharging a well ofr the aquifer. Rainwater harvesting can supplement water sources when they become scarce or are of low quality like brackish groundwater or polluted surface water in the rainy season. However, rainwater quality may be affected by air pollution, animal or bird droppings, insects, dirt and organic matter.
